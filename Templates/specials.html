All vegetarian food type

This meal is prepared for raw vegetable vegetarians; it is a combination of raw fruit and vegetables:

It may contain meat or poultry, fresh vegetables, packaged or frozen fruit and vegetables not processed with lactose, soy beverages as a substitute for milk, coffee creamers from non-dairy ingredients.

Jewish meals

This meal is for passengers who follow Jewish customs and are prepared with strict compliance of the Jewish dietary requirements.

These meals should be 48 hours before your scheduled departure and may not be available on all Kenya Airways flights. .

Hindu meals

This meal is for passengers wishing to follow Hindu customs and may contain one or more of the following ingredients

It contains; lamb, domestic fowl, fish, milk products, spicy foods, and curry.

Vegetarian Vegan Meal

This meal is prepared for vegan or pure vegetarian guests and does not contain any animal products or by products (eggs or dairy products)

It may contain high protein foods (lentils, beans, tofu), foods rich in iron (dried peas, beans, lentils, wholegrain products, dried apricots) and calcium (broccoli, mustard greens, kale, tofu, navy beans, dried figs, almonds, ground or in paste sesame seeds, brazil nuts, pistachio nuts).

Bland Meal

This meal is available for passengers suffering from stomach / intestinal problems.

It contains mostly soft foods that are easily digestible and without spices, such as mashed potatoes, fresh spinach, soft-boiled eggs, boiled meats, toast, milk, decaffeinated coffee, tea, and dairy products.
